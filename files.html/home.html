    Jamshoro Campus

    Mehran University of Engineering and Technology (MUET) is one of Pakistan's leading engineering institutions, located in Jamshoro. Established in 1963 as Sindh University Engineering College with just two departments, it has since grown into a prestigious university with over 35,000 alumni and 6,000 students.
